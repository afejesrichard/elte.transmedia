Degree Distribution & Power‑Law Fit (The Beast)
Interactive scatter of node degree (k) vs. frequency with a fitted “corrected” power‑law model; compare empirical distribution to the model line. Calculation method: Build histogram of node degrees; plot occurrences N(k). Fit model N(k) ≈ C·(k + k0)−n with parameters (k0=0.3; n=1.2), and overlay the expected values.
